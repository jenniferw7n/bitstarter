Design Ur Own Dress (DUOD) is a website / app for picturing, 3-D modeling and even creating your perfect dream dress.

We know every girl has a dream dress in her mind, for prom, wedding... and etc. Drawing with a pencil is an old fashion. Now we have a better idea for you!

The dress is like a puzzle, so first, you need to find the right pieces: For the basic sketch, We have various neckline, midriff, shoulder and silhouette options for you to choose from. You have all the freedom to combine and assemble the pieces into your own way.

For more details, you can also choose the right farbric, and plus, adding decoration in different styles, such as silk belt, bowknot, lace, embroidered gauze and...

You will be able to preview your final work in 3-D impact with details like a real garment, and you can create an unique dress which is totally following your own design pattern.
